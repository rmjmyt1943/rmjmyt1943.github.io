Origin and Meaning of Philo Sé

The Greek word ‘φιλ σε’ (Philo se) means ‘I love you.’ That was the Apostle Peter’s answer when Jesus asked him three times, “Do you love Me?” As we are inspired to spread and create a positive environment on campus, our focus is on delivering love.

Philo Sé Bible Study Club will show convincing proof that Science, World History, and other subjects that seem to contradict the Bible, support it.

Vision

The power of one person is weak, but if we all work together to solve the problems our campus is facing, the hopeful future of Mizzou will become a reality. We believe university students are the leaders of future generations who act for the real change of the Earth. When we come together centered on the love of a Mother, we can make a difference.
We will devote our efforts in helping change the present despair to future hope, and current frustration to future happiness on campus by getting more involved.
