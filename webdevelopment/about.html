Origin and Meaning of Philo Sé

The Greek word ‘φιλ σε’ (Philo sé) means ‘I love you.’ That was the Apostle Peter’s answer when Jesus asked him three times, “Do you love Me?” ( John 21:15-17 )

As we are inspired to spread and create a positive environment on campus, our focus is on delivering love.

Philo Sé Bible Study Club will show convincing proof that Science, World History, and other subjects that seem to contradict the Bible, support it. We use the original languages of the Bible i.e., Hebrew and Greek to authenticate the studies.
